It's been a few weeks since the last update, and I'm excited to finally deploy version 1.2! Special thanks to Felix, who encouraged me to add Spanish localization, which led to a refactor of almost every part of the app. That let me add the German localization, too.

I reached out to John Harper on Bsky and he gave me permission to add the Deep Cuts sheets, so they can also be found in this update.

New Languages

  • Added Spanish language localization and Spanish sheets.
  • Added German language localization and German sheets.

Deep Cuts Sheets

I've added Deep Cuts sheets to the app. Just toggle the sheet type in the Export step to see the Deep Cuts version of your sheet. Note: Descriptions and ability names aren't adjusted, so the only thing that changes is the sheet itself.

Fixes & Improvements

  • Introducing Scoundry Lab, the public staging area for experimental new features that you can try out before the next release.
  • Added font support for the sheet painter with several fonts to pick from. Font choices are stored with the character data so it'll be persistent when you share your character.
  • Selected ink colors are now stored with the character data.

Fixes and Improvements

  • Fix: Resolved text issues in the contacts and aliases fields.
  • Added an opt-in popup for tracking analytics. (This can be changed anytime from the new settings popup.)
  • Adjusted the styling on the scoundrel cards.
